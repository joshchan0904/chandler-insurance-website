What is General Liability Insurance?

General Liability (GL) insurance protects your business if someone is injured on your premises, your work causes property damage to a third party, or you are accused of personal injury (such as libel or slander). It is one of the most important coverages for almost every business.

Who Needs General Liability in Northern Minnesota?

General liability is essential for nearly every business, especially:

  • Contractors (roofing, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, flooring, excavation, etc.)
  • Retail stores, restaurants, and service businesses
  • Professional offices and consultants
  • Landscaping, snow removal, and property maintenance companies
  • Manufacturers and wholesalers
  • Any business that interacts with customers or the public
