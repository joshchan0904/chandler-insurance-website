What is Commercial Auto Insurance?

Commercial auto insurance covers vehicles used for business purposes. It goes beyond personal auto policies and includes liability for business use, physical damage to your work vehicles, and often hired/non-owned auto coverage when employees drive their own cars for work.

Who Needs Commercial Auto in Northern Minnesota?

  • Construction contractors with work trucks and equipment haulers
  • Delivery services, couriers, and last-mile businesses
  • Landscaping, snow removal, and property maintenance companies
  • Plumbers, electricians, HVAC, and other trade contractors
  • Retail and wholesale businesses with delivery or service vehicles
  • Any company whose employees drive for work

Why Local Expertise Matters for Commercial Auto

Northern Minnesota roads, harsh winters, and remote job sites create unique risks. We understand these challenges and work with carriers experienced in insuring contractors and service businesses in our area. We can help with:
