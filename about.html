About Chandler Insurance Agency

Proudly serving families and businesses across all of Minnesota since 1977.

Our Story

Founded in 1977, Chandler Insurance Agency has deep roots across Minnesota. In 2018, owner Joe Chandler acquired the agency from Bob Kuschel, expanding its legacy of protecting local communities. With the recent addition of Hegland Insurance Agency customers, we've grown our offerings while maintaining our commitment to advocacy, seamless claims support, and finding the best solutions from multiple carriers. Whether it's auto, home, commercial, or life insurance, we're here to safeguard what matters most to you — anywhere in Minnesota.

Meet Our Team
