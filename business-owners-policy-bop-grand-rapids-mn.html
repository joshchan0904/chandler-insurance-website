What is a Business Owners Policy (BOP)?

A Business Owners Policy (BOP) is a convenient, cost-effective insurance package that combines general liability and commercial property coverage into one policy. It is designed for small to medium-sized businesses and often includes business income coverage as well.

Is a BOP Right for Your Business?

BOPs are a great fit for many businesses in Grand Rapids and Itasca County, including:

  • Retail stores, shops, and boutiques
  • Restaurants, cafes, and food service businesses
  • Contractors and tradespeople (with certain limitations)
  • Professional offices and service businesses
  • Wholesale and light manufacturing operations

Note: Some higher-risk businesses (certain contractors, bars, etc.) may need separate general liability and property policies instead of a BOP. We can help you determine the best option.
