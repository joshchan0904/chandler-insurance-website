What is Recreational Vehicle Insurance?

Recreational Vehicle (RV) insurance protects your ATV, snowmobile, boat, camper, motorcycle, dirt bike, and other fun vehicles. In Minnesota, where outdoor recreation is a way of life, having the right coverage for your toys is just as important as insuring your daily driver. Whether you ride trails in the Superior National Forest, boat on the lakes, or camp in the Boundary Waters area, we have specialized policies designed for the Minnesota lifestyle.

How does RV Insurance work?

Recreational vehicle insurance works similarly to auto insurance but is tailored for seasonal and specialty vehicles. You can choose liability-only coverage or add physical damage protection (comprehensive and collision). Many policies also include roadside assistance, rental reimbursement, and storage coverage for when your vehicle is not in use during the off-season. We customize policies based on how and when you use your recreational vehicles.

Why is RV Insurance important in Minnesota?

Minnesota has some of the best recreational opportunities in the country — but also some of the harshest conditions. Snowmobiles and ATVs face risks from trail accidents, tree damage, and theft. Boats are vulnerable to storms, submerged objects, and winter storage damage. Campers and RVs can be damaged by hail, wind, or falling branches. Without proper coverage, a single incident could cost you thousands of dollars.

What coverages are available?

  • Liability Coverage: Protects you if you're at fault in an accident involving others
  • Collision Coverage: Repairs your vehicle after an accident, regardless of fault
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Covers theft, vandalism, fire, hail, and falling objects
  • Roadside Assistance: Towing, jump starts, lockout service, and more
  • Storage Coverage: Protects your vehicle while it's stored for the winter
  • Personal Effects Coverage: Covers gear and equipment inside your RV
  • Liability for Guests: Important for boats and campers with passengers

FAQs about Recreational Vehicle Insurance

Do I need insurance for my snowmobile or ATV?

While not always legally required, it's highly recommended. Many trails and public lands require proof of insurance. More importantly, accidents involving recreational vehicles can result in serious injuries and expensive claims.

Can I insure my boat for the winter?

Yes. We offer storage-only policies at a reduced rate while your boat is winterized and stored. You can also add coverage for shrink-wrapping and indoor storage facilities.

Does my homeowners policy cover my camper?

Usually not. Most homeowners policies have very limited or no coverage for recreational vehicles. A separate RV policy is almost always better and more affordable.

Can I get a discount for safe driving?

Yes. We offer discounts for clean driving records, completing safety courses (especially for snowmobiles and boats), and bundling multiple recreational vehicles together.

What if I only use my RV a few weekends a year?

We can customize your policy with higher deductibles and seasonal usage discounts to keep your premiums as low as possible while still protecting your investment.
